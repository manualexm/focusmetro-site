Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

The short version: FocusMetro does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What Data We Collect

None. FocusMetro does not collect any personal information, usage analytics, or tracking data.

Data Stored on Your Device

FocusMetro stores the following data locally on your device to provide its features:

  • Focus session history (duration, category, station names)
  • App preferences (sound settings, map style)
  • Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling FocusMetro.

In-App Purchases

FocusMetro offers optional tip jar purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Third-Party Services

FocusMetro does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app uses Apple MapKit to display maps, which is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

FocusMetro does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since the app does not collect any personal data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
